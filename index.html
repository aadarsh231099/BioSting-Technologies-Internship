Biosting Technologies was founded in the year 2019 as a Techno-Commercial Agency.

  • We are experts in the design, supply, commissioning, and validation of Bio safety laboratories like Negative Pressure Rooms, Positive Pressure Rooms, and Modular Operation Theatres, among other things.
  • We address the needs of our clientele by sourcing items from a network of well-established manufacturers in India.

We have earned our clients' respect by high levels of responsiveness, integrity, business ethics, and commitment to quality and service.

Mission

Biosting Technologies strives for the fulfillment of below stated missions.

To design, commission, and maintain state of art infrastructure to high priority areas in hospitals, medical laboratories and pharma industries.

To develop and maintain end to end electronic medical records for hospitals and laboratories

Vision

To provide high impact eco-friendly infra-structure/IT-solutions for health care sectors.
